Yoga And Exercise

Our yoga and exercise sections are designed to be accessible to both patients and normal individuals with busy daily schedules. We provide resources on yoga and exercise practices that can be incorporated into daily life, including short routines that can be done at home or at work.

For patients, we offer yoga and exercise practices that are gentle and appropriate for different medical conditions. Our resources include information on modifications and props that can be used to make practices safe and comfortable.

For normal individuals, we provide resources on how to build an effective exercise routine and how to maintain motivation for long-term health benefits. Our goal is to provide practical and achievable strategies that fit into busy schedules.

Whether you're a patient looking to improve your health or a busy individual trying to maintain wellness, our yoga and exercise sections offer resources that can help you achieve your goals.
